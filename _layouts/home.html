My long story, short.

I'm a Designer based in Barcelona who loves sports and has been traveling around the world the whole 2017. Me & my partner started in Rusia and will finish in San Francisco.

Before that decision, I was working at Lola [Mullenlowe Group] as a Lead Designer of a team of 3 to 4 other designers. Even with a group that small, we got a really big project in our hands: Rethink & Improve the Global Digital Ecosystem of the largest Spanish automotive brand, SEAT.

Previously, I worked for a really cool company called Mobivery. There I crafted tons of mobile applications for all the clients & platforms you can imagine. Also there, I had the opportunity to work on Malcom, a SaaS for Mobile Marketing that combined push, analytics and a layer of BI.

In the side, I taught design in schools like Ironhack or TheAppSchool; and directly to design teams inside companies like Schneider Electrics or TV3.
