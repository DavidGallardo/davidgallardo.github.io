My long story, short.

I'm a Designer based in Barcelona who loves sports and has been traveling the world during 2017. My couple and I started in St.Petersburg and finished in San Francisco.

Before that decision, I was working at LOLA [Mullenlowe Group] leading a small team of 3 to 4 other designers. Even with a group that small, we got a really big project in our hands: Rethink & Improve the Global Digital Ecosystem of the largest Spanish automotive brand, SEAT.

Even before, I worked for a really cool company called Mobivery. There I crafted tons of mobile applications for all the clients and platforms you can imagine. Also, I had the opportunity to work on Malcom, a SaaS for Mobile Marketing that combined push, analytics and a layer of BI.

In the side, I taught design in schools like Ironhack or TheAppSchool and directly to design teams inside companies like Schneider Electrics or TV3.
